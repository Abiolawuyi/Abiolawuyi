My Impactful Portfolio Projects

This section is dedicated to showcasing how I translate complex requirements into real-world, high-impact Salesforce solutions. Each project highlights my specific contributions, the innovative solutions implemented across Service Cloud and Field Service Lightning, and the quantifiable results achieved.

Hinch Services (FSL Implementation)

Led the comprehensive Salesforce Field Service Lightning implementation for Hinch Services, modernizing their CRM, enhancing data management, and streamlining customer service operations. Key contributions include:

  • Orchestrated the complete FSL managed package setup, including Operating Hours, Service Resources, and robust Team configurations.
  • Engineered intelligent scheduling policies with "Grade A expertise" and resource availability, optimizing dispatch through custom Gantt chart views and a critical "In Jeopardy" conflict detection palette.
  • Implemented dynamic VIP and first-time customer flagging on appointments, providing dispatchers with instant, actionable insights.
  • Developed seamless mobile-first functionalities for technicians, enabling on-the-spot booking, product requests, asset photo capture, and comprehensive service report generation with digital signatures.
  • Established proactive maintenance plans and automated bi-weekly timesheet/approval processes, significantly enhancing operational oversight and efficiency.

ABC Electronics (FSL Optimization)

Engaged as a consultant to address key pain points for ABC Electronics, optimizing their field service operations, improving technician productivity, and enhancing customer experiences nationwide. The solution focused on:

  • Deployed advanced FSL scheduling logic, eradicating double bookings and dramatically optimizing technician dispatch based on dynamic travel times and skills.
  • Engineered robust communication channels and real-time location/job status dashboards, empowering dispatchers with unprecedented resource allocation efficiency and proactive issue resolution.
  • Centralized and automated work order processes, eliminating data errors, ensuring comprehensive service instructions, and optimizing documentation.
  • Established powerful, intuitive dashboards to meticulously track KPIs, providing actionable insights for continuous service improvement and strategic decision-making.
  • Customized field worker visibility to provide optimal access.

The Petiterra Project: BTS Motors (Service Cloud)

Implemented Salesforce Service Cloud solutions to streamline support processes for BTS Motors, enhancing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency from fragmented manual systems to a unified cloud-based application. Key contributions include:

  • Engineered automated Email-to-Case and Web-to-Case features, coupled with dynamic Queue Omni Routing.
  • Established robust Service Contracts and meticulously configured Entitlements/Entitlement Processes.
  • Developed a comprehensive Community Site integrated with an intuitive Knowledge Base.
  • Implemented advanced reporting and analytics to track cases by department and maintenance frequency.
  • Designed and enforced stringent approval workflows for case closure by Customer Service supervisors, ensuring data integrity and accountability.
  • Produced comprehensive Standard Operating Procedures and Training Manuals, ensuring rapid user adoption and long-term solution sustainability.
